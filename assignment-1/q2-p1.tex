\subsection*{(i)}
\[
	x_1 + x_2 + 2x_3 = 8
\]
\[
	-x_1 - 2x_2 + 3x_3 = 1
\]
\[
	3x_1 - 7x_2 + 4x_3 = 10
\]

\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		1  & 1  & 2 & 8  \\
		-1 & -2 & 3 & 1  \\
		3  & -7 & 4 & 10
	\end{pmatrix}
\]

$R_2 + R_1$:
\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		1 & 1   & 2  & 8   \\
		0 & -1  & 5  & 9   \\
		3  & -7 & 4 & 10
	\end{pmatrix}
\]

$R_3 - 3R_1$:
\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		1 & 1   & 2  & 8   \\
		0 & -1  & 5  & 9   \\
		0 & -10 & -2 & -14
	\end{pmatrix}
\]

$R_2 \times (-1)$:
\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		1 & 1   & 2  & 8   \\
		0 & 1   & -5 & -9  \\
		0 & -10 & -2 & -14
	\end{pmatrix}
\]

$R_3 + 10R_2$:
\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		1 & 1 & 2   & 8    \\
		0 & 1 & -5  & -9   \\
		0 & 0 & -52 & -104
	\end{pmatrix}
\]

$R_3 \times (-\frac{1}{52})$:
\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		1 & 1 & 2  & 8  \\
		0 & 1 & -5 & -9 \\
		0 & 0 & 1  & 2
	\end{pmatrix}
\]

Back substitution:
\[
	x_3 = 2
\]
\[
	x_2 - 5x_3 = -9 \Rightarrow x_2 = -9 + 5(2) = 1
\]
\[
	x_1 + x_2 + 2x_3 = 8 \Rightarrow x_1 = 8 - 1 - 2(2) = 3
\]

Therefore: $x_1 = 3, x_2 = 1, x_3 = 2$
