\subsection*{(ii)}
\begin{align*}
	2x_1 + 2x_2 + 2x_3  & = 0  \\
	-2x_1 + 5x_2 + 2x_3 & = 1  \\
	8x_1 + x_2 + 4x_3   & = -1
\end{align*}

\[
	\left(\begin{array}{ccc|c}
			2  & 2 & 2 & 0  \\
			-2 & 5 & 2 & 1  \\
			8  & 1 & 4 & -1
		\end{array}\right)
\]

$R_1 \times \frac{1}{2}$:
\[
	\left(\begin{array}{ccc|c}
			1  & 1 & 1 & 0  \\
			-2 & 5 & 2 & 1  \\
			8  & 1 & 4 & -1
		\end{array}\right)
\]

$R_2 + 2R_1$:
\[
	\left(\begin{array}{ccc|c}
			1 & 1  & 1  & 0  \\
			0 & 7  & 4  & 1  \\
			8  & 1 & 4 & -1
		\end{array}\right)
\]

$R_3 - 8R_1$:
\[
	\left(\begin{array}{ccc|c}
			1 & 1  & 1  & 0  \\
			0 & 7  & 4  & 1  \\
			0 & -7 & -4 & -1
		\end{array}\right)
\]

$R_3 + R_2$:
\[
	\left(\begin{array}{ccc|c}
			1 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\
			0 & 7 & 4 & 1 \\
			0 & 0 & 0 & 0
		\end{array}\right)
\]

$R_2 \times \frac{1}{7}$:
\[
	\left(\begin{array}{ccc|c}
			1 & 1 & 1           & 0           \\
			0 & 1 & \frac{4}{7} & \frac{1}{7} \\
			0 & 0 & 0           & 0
		\end{array}\right)
\]

Let $x_3 = t$. From row 2: $x_2 + \frac{4t}{7} = \frac{1}{7} \Rightarrow x_2 = \frac{1-4t}{7}$

From row 1: $x_1 + x_2 + x_3 = 0 \Rightarrow x_1 = -\frac{1-4t}{7} - t = \frac{-1-3t}{7}$

$\boxed{(x_1, x_2, x_3) = \left(\frac{-1-3t}{7}, \frac{1-4t}{7}, t\right)}$
