\[
	\begin{pmatrix}
		-1 & 3 & -4 \\
		2  & 4 & 1  \\
		-4 & 2 & -9
	\end{pmatrix}
\]

$\det(A) = -1(4 \cdot (-9) - 1 \cdot 2) - 3(2 \cdot (-9) - 1 \cdot (-4)) + (-4)(2 \cdot 2 - 4 \cdot (-4))$
$= -1(-36 - 2) - 3(-18 + 4) - 4(4 + 16)$
$= -1(-38) - 3(-14) - 4(20)$
$= 38 + 42 - 80 = 0$

The matrix is not invertible (singular).
